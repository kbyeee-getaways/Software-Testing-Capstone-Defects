About Us

A small bakery with a big commitment to doing things the right way.

Our Story

Honey & Crumb was founded in 2019 by Clara and James Whitfield, a couple who spent years baking out of their home kitchen before finally opening the doors to a proper storefront on Maple Street.

What started as a weekend sourdough habit became a full-blown obsession with fermentation, sourcing, and craft. Clara trained as a pastry chef in Lyon. James spent three years apprenticing at a wood-fired bakery in Vermont. Together, they built something that feels genuinely rooted in this community.

🏡

How We Bake

Everything starts the night before. Doughs are mixed, shaped, and cold-proofed overnight so they hit the oven at 5am. By the time we open at 7, the bread is out of the oven and the pastries are just finishing.

We use no preservatives, no dough conditioners, and no shortcuts. If something runs out, it runs out. We'd rather sell less and bake it right.

🕯️

What We Stand For

🌱

Local First
